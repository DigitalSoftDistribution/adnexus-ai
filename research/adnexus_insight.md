## Insight 3: The "Creative Cascade" — Cross-Platform Fatigue as Differentiator
**Derived From**: Dim 03 (Creative Intelligence), Dim 06 (Morning Brief), Dim 07 (Competitive Intel)
**Rationale**: Creative fatigue lifecycles differ by platform (TikTok 3-7 days, Meta 1-2 weeks, Google varies). No tool tracks fatigue ACROSS platforms. But fatigue cascades — a TikTok creative that dies signals the same concept is aging on Meta too. Combining: (a) unified fatigue detection, (b) cascade prediction, (c) competitive creative monitoring (what competitors are testing), (d) refresh recommendations in the Morning Brief = a **creative intelligence flywheel** no competitor has.
**Implications**: This becomes the "unsolvable problem" that drives switching from Madgicx (Meta-only creative intel). Premium feature at $199+ tier.
**Confidence**: High
